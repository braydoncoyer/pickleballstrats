# Content Safety Rules

These rules apply to ALL content generation in this project.

## Blocked Content Categories

### 1. Medical/Health Advice
- Diagnosing medical conditions
- Recommending treatments or medications
- Making health claims about supplements
- Providing mental health treatment advice
- "Curing" any condition

### 2. Financial/Investment Advice
- Recommending specific investments
- Stock picks or trading strategies
- Cryptocurrency investment advice
- "Get rich quick" content
- Specific financial planning advice

### 3. Legal Advice
- Providing legal recommendations
- How to handle legal situations
- Lawsuit or litigation strategy
- Avoiding legal consequences

### 4. War/Violence/Crime
- Detailed descriptions of violence
- Instructions for harmful activities
- Weapons or explosives information
- Criminal how-to content

### 5. Political Controversy
- Partisan political content
- Election-related controversy
- Inflammatory political takes
- Conspiracy theories

### 6. Other Blocked Content
- Adult/sexual content
- Gambling strategies
- Drug use or production
- Weapon modifications

## Allowed Content

✅ General wellness and self-improvement
✅ Productivity and time management
✅ Technology tutorials and guides
✅ Career development advice
✅ Creative skills and hobbies
✅ Business and entrepreneurship (general)
✅ Educational content
✅ Cooking and recipes
✅ Travel guides and tips
✅ Home improvement (general)

## Enforcement

1. All topics are validated before generation
2. All content is checked before publication
3. Blocked content is logged for review
4. No exceptions without human approval

## When in Doubt

If a topic seems borderline:
1. Mark as unsafe
2. Log for human review
3. Suggest safer alternatives

Protecting ad accounts is more important than publishing one article.
